# Interview Checklist

Use these prompts only when the answer is not already in `knowledge/`.

## Job Fit

- Which requirements are must-have, and where is the strongest existing evidence?
- Which requirements are weak or missing, and how should they be handled honestly?
- What words from the job posting should appear naturally in the CV?

## Evidence

- What scale did you own: team size, budget, users, services, transactions, regions, uptime, or incident volume?
- What measurable outcomes can be stated safely?
- Which executive, regulatory, audit, vendor, customer, or cross-functional stakeholders were involved?
- Which decisions show judgment under constraints?

## Positioning

- What should the CV lead with for this role: leadership, domain expertise, technical depth, delivery, operations, research, product impact, customer impact, or another strength?
- Which older experience should stay short?
- Which side projects strengthen this application?

## Knowledge Updates

- Add durable facts to `knowledge/`.
- Keep job-specific phrasing and strategy in `applications/<slug>/strategy.md`.
- Record raw interview notes in `applications/<slug>/interview.md`.
